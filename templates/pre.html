Opportunity

engages in business with these vendors atleast once a week

cyber presence
Opportunity-II
organizing location/item/review/availability information
for these vendors will be a tremendous resource
Plus vendors will get online exposure which will increase
their business. A win-win.
Discovery of good/reliable hawkers is mostly by word of 
mouth. We can change that
Safety issues at these places can be reported almost
instantly
Problem
These vendors are truly "mobile". have irregular schedules
which are dependent on footfall, weather etc
currently users just visit on a guessed best time. The vendor
could have left, not arrived or ran out of stuff.
Solution
Almost all vendors have mobile/sms. data may be but lets
not rely on that.

Only effort from vendors is to just send a free sms to
indicate arrival/departure/holidays and a one time 
registration

Data/advertisement is taken care by AaloMe team/users

Have scouts go and register vendors. Collect vendor data
launch in one city, have a bunch of reviews by users. As soon
as we hit a critical mass arrange a food fair for all the top
vendors.

Rinse, repeat in a different city
